Building the world’s first ethical, transparent, and legally compliant dataset for open-source AI training.

Read the Manifesto

Our 5 Core Principles

Transparency

We ensure all data sources, processes, and decisions are open and accessible for scrutiny and understanding.

Fairness & Non-Discrimination

We actively prevent bias and promote equal treatment in all aspects of dataset creation and use.

Privacy & Data Governance

We respect individual privacy and uphold the highest standards in data protection and governance.

Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions, ensuring ethical conduct and redress for any harm caused.

Social & Environmental Benefit

We prioritize positive impact on society and the environment in all our initiatives.
