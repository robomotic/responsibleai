Read the Manifesto

Key Points of the Manifesto

  • Vision: Build the world’s first ethical, transparent, and legally compliant dataset for open-source AI training.
  • Core Principles: Copyright respect, fair compensation, transparency, ethical data selection, governance, and open technical standards.
  • Legal Alignment: Complies with OECD AI Principles, EU AI Act, UK AI Safety, and international copyright law.
  • Enhanced robots.txt Protocol: Proposes a new standard for website owners to control AI training permissions, including content type, commercial use, attribution, compensation, and time limits.
  • Implementation Roadmap: Develop tools, pilot with early adopters, scale, and foster a community ecosystem.
