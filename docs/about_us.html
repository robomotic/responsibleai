Responsible AI Dataset Initiative is in the process of registering as a Charitable Incorporated Organization (CIO) in England, United Kingdom. Our mission is to advance the public good by building the worldâ€™s first ethical, transparent, and legally compliant dataset for open-source AI training.

Our Legal Structure

We have chosen the CIO structure for its public benefit focus, strong governance, and ability to attract both public and private support. As a CIO, we will be regulated by the Charity Commission for England and Wales, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Governance & Board

  • Governed by a board of trustees and expert advisors in AI, ethics, law, and technology
  • Independence from commercial interests, with clear conflict of interest policies
  • Commitment to open, transparent decision-making

Funding & Public Benefit

  • Funded by grants, donations, and sponsorships from individuals and organizations
  • All resources are dedicated to public benefit and the advancement of responsible AI
  • We welcome support from the global technology community, including companies, researchers, and the public
