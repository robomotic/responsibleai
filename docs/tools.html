Copyright Protection Tools for Your Website

Anubis

https://github.com/TecharoHQ/anubis

How it protects your copyright website:
Anubis is an open-source tool designed to detect and block web scrapers and automated bots from accessing your website. By integrating Anubis, you can monitor incoming traffic, identify suspicious scraping activity, and automatically block or challenge bots that attempt to copy your site's content. This helps prevent unauthorized data extraction and protects your copyrighted material from being harvested by large language model (LLM) training datasets or other third parties.

Copyright Traps

https://github.com/computationalprivacy/copyright-traps

How it protects your copyright website:
The Copyright Traps toolkit allows you to embed unique, hard-to-detect sequences ("copyright traps") into your website's content. If these sequences later appear in outputs from large language models or other AI systems, it provides strong evidence that your content was used in their training data. This can help you audit for copyright infringement and enforce your rights if your protected material is misused by AI companies or data scrapers.
